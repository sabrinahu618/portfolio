A Bit About Me

My love for technology and problem-solving took off with my first invention to help my community during hurricanes. I spent that year designing, building, testing, failing, and trying over and over again. I became enamored with the process of turning ideas and possibilities into impact.

Fast forward to today... that spark is still going. These past few months, I've been working at the Digital Humanities Lab to build a communication platform for exiled Ukrainian scientists. I've learned how to build websites (like the one you're on right now!), design apps, and create delightful user experiences. Of course, the learning never stops. I'm excited to continue growing, especially in the intersection of humanities and STEM.

Beyond this, you can spot me in the wild munching on honey lavender ice cream, strumming my ukulele, sipping hot matcha, or dramatically staring off into space.
